This Is Emily Zhao



    She is a student at San Francisco State University, currently pursuing a Bachelor’s degree in Visual Communication along with a minor in Comic Studies. She transferred there from West Valley College where she spent three years there after graduating from Lynbrook High School. She is passionate about arts and crafts and even owns her own Etsy shop, where she has already made hundreds of sales there. Ever since middle school, she has always loved being creative and knew she wanted to pursue a career related to the arts. Eventually, she decided to learn how to design digitally due to the technological advancements of today. Right now, she is working on acquiring all of the necessary GPA points for Visual Communication, and once she is done with that, she’ll work on her Comic Studies. Her biggest dream is to make her own superhero comics, like Stan Lee. Right now, she is honing her drawing skills and researching on how to make comics. She has already made up quite a few superheroes and villains, such as Majestic Girl and the Collector, so now she has to learn how to actually make the comics. She has a solid foundation in Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign, and is currently learning to draw on an app on her iPad called ibisPaint X after it was recommended to her by someone she met in the Comics Club at San Francisco State University. Though she loves arts and crafts, most of her creative activities in the past have been making pendant necklaces, fashion accessories and customizing Littlest Pet Shop toys, therefore drawing is a skill she is still honing as of now.
